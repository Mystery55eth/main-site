Discovery Of The Four Keys

The young prince discovers the existence of the four keys - bronze, silver, gold, and queen - each with its own unique power.

After wandering through the wilderness, the prince discovers a great stone gate guarded by ancient warriors. He uses the queen key to unlock the gate, revealing a small, ornate box holding the greatest mystery of all.
